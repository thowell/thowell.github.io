Hello, my name is Taylor Howell.

Currently, I am a Research Scientist intern at DeepMind and PhD candidate at Stanford University. At DeepMind, I work on the MuJoCo team with Yuval Tassa and Zachary Manchester in the Robotic Exploration Lab on motion planning for underactuated robotics systems. I'm developing tools for and fast contact-implicit model-predictive control and differentiable simulators for robotics.

Previously, I interned at Google Brain with Vikas Sindhwani working on differentiable optimization-based dynamics for robot motion planning. Prior to that, I worked on control for magnetically actuated microrobots with Jake Abbott at the Telerobotics Lab and on a personalized medicine system for ovarian-cancer treatments with Bruce Gale at the State of Utah Center of Excellence for Biomedical Microfluidics. I also spent the summer after finishing my bachelor's degree sharing my love for robotics by teaching kids how to build and program robots at University of Utah GREAT camp.

I love traveling, cinema, and making things.
